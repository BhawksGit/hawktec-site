R4xMP Setup & Troubleshooting

R4xMP Details

  • Simple plug and play — just add your own ROM files
  • Compatible with Nintendo DS, DS Lite, DSi, 2DS, and 3DS
  • No timebomb — no expiration date

Quick Start

  1. Download the R4xMP firmware and extract it onto a FAT32-formatted micro SD:
    Download R4xMP FirmwareVersion Ace3DS+_R4iLS_Wood_R4_1.62
  2. Insert micro SD into the R4xMP, insert the cart into your DS.
  3. Power on — the R4xMP menu should load.

SD Card Requirements

The R4xMP does not include a micro SD card. The card must be formatted as FAT32. Cards 32 GB or smaller can be formatted directly through your operating system. Cards larger than 32 GB require a third-party formatting tool on Windows — Rufus is a commonly recommended option.

Expected File Structure

After extraction, your SD root should look like:

microSD/ ├── __rpg/ ├── GBA ROMS/ ├── GBC ROMS/ ├── NDS ROMS/ ├── NES ROMS/ ├── _DS_MENU.DAT ├── _DSMENU.DAT └── README
⚠

These files must be in the root directory of your micro SD card, not inside any folders. Often, these files end up in a subfolder when extracting. Move them from the folder to the main directory of the micro SD card to resolve the issue.

FAQ

"Can't open _dsmenu.dat"

Ensure _DSMENU.DAT, _DS_MENU.DAT, and the __rpg folder are in the root of your SD — not inside subfolders.

"SD/TF card error"

Reformat the micro SD card to FAT32 using a full format (not Quick Format). Use a card rated Class 10 or higher, 32 GB or smaller.

I'm seeing a black screen or error when I try to use the R4!

Make sure the firmware files are extracted to the root of the SD card, not inside a subfolder. Also verify the card is formatted as FAT32 and is 32 GB or smaller.

Does this have a timebomb?

No. The R4xMP will not expire.

Can I use Twilight Menu++?

Yes — many users prefer it. Guides are available on YouTube and the Twilight Menu++ GitHub.
