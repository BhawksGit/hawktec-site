R4xMP Setup & Troubleshooting

Everything you need to get your HawkTec R4xMP up and running.

Quick Start

  1. Get a micro SD card — 32 GB or smaller, formatted FAT32.
  2. Download the R4xMP kernel from the link on your packaging, or from r4inp.com.
  3. Extract the zip directly onto the root of the micro SD.
  4. Insert the micro SD into the R4xMP, insert the cart into your DS.
  5. Power on — the R4xMP menu should load.

SD Card Requirements

The R4xMP does not include a micro SD card. Use a standard micro SDHC card, 32 GB max, formatted FAT32. Larger cards need reformatting via a tool like guiformat.

Expected File Structure

After extraction, your SD root should look like:

microSD/ ├── _DSMENU.DAT ├── _DS_MENU.DAT └── _rpg/ └── ...

FAQ

"Can't open _dsmenu.dat"

Ensure _DSMENU.DAT, _DS_MENU.DAT, and the _rpg folder are in the root of your SD — not inside subfolders.

Does this have a timebomb?

No. The R4xMP will not expire.

Can I use Twilight Menu++?

Yes — many users prefer it. Guides are available on YouTube and the Twilight Menu++ GitHub.

What SD size should I use?

32 GB or smaller. Larger cards need FAT32 formatting.
